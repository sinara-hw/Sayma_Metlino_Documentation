.. _metlino_FMC_section:

FMC
===

* VADJ: 1.8 V @ 2 A
* FPGA Banks: 66HP and 68HP

The connector is compliant with ANSI/VITA 57.1 FMC-LPC Standard.

.. note:: DP4 is disconnected by default (see :ref:`metlino_transceiver_connections`).
